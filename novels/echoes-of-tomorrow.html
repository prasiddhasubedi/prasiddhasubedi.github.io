About This Novel

Echoes of Tomorrow is a thought-provoking science fiction thriller that explores the paradoxes of time communication and the weight of knowledge about the future. Drawing inspiration from quantum mechanics and the philosophical implications of predestination versus free will, the novel weaves together hard science and human drama.

The story challenges readers to consider: If you could receive messages from your future self, would you follow them? And more importantly, what would you be willing to sacrifice to change tomorrow?

Themes: Time paradoxes, free will vs. determinism, scientific ethics, trust and betrayal, the cost of knowledge

Target Audience: Fans of hard sci-fi, thriller enthusiasts, readers who enjoy philosophical depth alongside action
