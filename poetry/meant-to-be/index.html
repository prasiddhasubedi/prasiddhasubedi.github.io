Meant To Be

PRASIDDHA
February 11, 2026

Some things arrive quietly,

like a breeze

slipping through an open window.


You barely notice at first

then, somehow,

they settle into the shape of your day.


No alarms, no fanfare,

just a sense that

this belongs.


The conversation that stretches

without effort.

The silence that isn’t empty.


The laugh that catches your chest

in exactly the right way.

You realize, slowly,

that it wasn’t about searching,

or forcing the fit.


It was already here,

waiting for the right moment.


And when you look back,

you see the lines all converged,

not by chance,

but by something

gentle, exact,

and inevitable.
