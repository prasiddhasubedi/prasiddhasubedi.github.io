SHE, LIKE EVERYTHING BEAUTIFUL

prasiddha
December 15, 2025

She, like everything beautiful,

Comes with thorns and gentle rain;

A rose that blooms in morning light,

Yet fades when touched by evening's pain.

She, like everything beautiful,

Is fleeting, fragile, and sublime;

A sunset painted in the sky,

That vanishes with passing time.

She, like everything beautiful,

Demands attention, care, and grace;

A melody that haunts the soul,

A memory time cannot erase.

She, like everything beautiful,

Is worth the risk, the fear, the fall;

For beauty, though it may not last,

Is the most precious gift of all.
