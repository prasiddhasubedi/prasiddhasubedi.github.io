IF STARS HAD WINDOWS

prasiddha

If stars had windows, what would they see?

The dreams of lovers beneath their tree?

The lonely wanderer's silent plea?

The child who wishes to be free?

If stars had windows, would they watch

As lives unfold, as hearts unlatch?

Would they witness joy and sorrow blend,

Or turn away when stories end?

If stars had windows, could they tell

Of futures bright or dark as well?

Would they share their ancient sight,

Or keep their secrets in the night?

If stars had windows, I would climb

Beyond the boundaries of time;

I'd peer inside and finally know

What makes the universe aglow.
