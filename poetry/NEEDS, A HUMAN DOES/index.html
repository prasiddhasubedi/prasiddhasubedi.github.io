NEEDS, A HUMAN DOES

prasiddha
January 6, 2026

A human needs to be held close,

To feel a heartbeat, not a ghost;

To know that someone understands,

The weight we carry in our hands.

A human needs a place to rest,

Where fears subside and we're our best;

A shelter built not just of stone,

But love that makes a house a home.

A human needs to dream and hope,

To climb the mountains, find the rope;

To see beyond the present pain,

And trust that sun will come with rain.

A human needs to give and take,

To love, to lose, to bend, to break;

For in our needs, we're most alive—

Connected, human, born to thrive.
