MAYBE SOMEDAY

prasiddha

Maybe someday the sun will rise

Without the weight of yesterday's goodbyes;

Maybe someday the rain will fall

Without echoing your distant call.

Maybe someday I'll walk alone

Without your shadow, fully grown;

Maybe someday these wounds will heal,

And I'll remember how to feel.

Maybe someday the stars will shine

Without reminding me you were mine;

Maybe someday this aching heart

Will learn to live, to make a start.

Maybe someday is all I have,

A fragile hope, a gentle salve;

But hope is all we need, they say,

To make it through another day.
