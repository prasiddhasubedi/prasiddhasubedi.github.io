Beauty Crisis Report

Published: December 2025 | Category: Poetry

About This Poem

"My First Poem" represents the beginning of a creative journey. It explores the vulnerability and excitement of starting something new, particularly in the realm of creative writing. The poem uses imagery of dawn, journeys, and transformation to symbolize the growth that comes with embracing one's creative voice.

This piece is structured in four quatrains with an ABAB rhyme scheme, reflecting traditional poetic forms while conveying a personal and contemporary message about the power of self-expression through words.
